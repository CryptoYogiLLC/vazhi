## Full Description (English)

VAZHI (வழி) is your free, offline Tamil assistant that protects you from scams, connects you with government benefits, shares Tamil culture and wisdom, and guides your education — all without internet, without tracking, without fees.

### Scam Protection & Cyber Safety
Stay safe from 43+ known scam patterns targeting Tamil Nadu residents. Get instant alerts about banking fraud, UPI scams, fake government messages, phone scams, and more. Each scam includes red flags to watch for, real examples, and how to report. Plus 27 cyber safety tips to protect your family online.

### Thirukkural & Tamil Culture
All 1,330 Thirukkural verses with Tamil meanings and English translations. Learn about the 18 Siddhars, their teachings and contributions. Explore 53 Tamil festivals with rituals and significance. Search by verse number, athikaram, or keyword.

### Government Schemes & Legal Rights
Access 38+ government welfare schemes (TN State + Central) with eligibility criteria, required documents, and how to apply. Know your 33 legal rights across fundamental, consumer, women's, worker, and property categories. Use 15 ready-made legal templates for RTI applications, FIR requests, consumer complaints, and more.

### Education & Career
Browse 33 scholarships from TN government, central government, and private organizations. Get details on 23 competitive exams — NEET, JEE, TNPSC, UPSC, banking, and more — with eligibility, syllabus links, and important dates.

### Healthcare & Emergency
Find hospitals across Tamil Nadu with contact numbers and insurance acceptance details. Access 41 Siddha medicine remedies for common ailments. Quickly reach 31 emergency contacts including police, ambulance, cyber crime helpline, and women's helpline.
